About UniRareAI

This Unified Rare Disease AI platform helps analyze medical records using AI to identify rare diseases

Key Features

1. Upload clinical or medical records to be analyzed by AI and detect rare diseases based on common patterns in the database

2. Records are organized and shared in real time with certified healthcare professionals and scientists

3. Provides healthcare professionals the Standards of Care Guidelines that should be followed to consistently treat diagnosed disease accurately

4. Connect patients to supporting organizations, rare disease specialists, and a network of patients with a similar disease
