About UniRareAI

UniRareAI is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to assist medical professionals in identifying rare diseases. By analyzing clinical records, UniRareAI extracts key medical entities and provides standard-of-care guidelines for rare conditions.

How It Works

  • Upload medical or clinical records in text format.
  • The AI model analyzes the text to detect relevant medical entities.
  • For rare diseases, the platform provides standard-of-care guidelines.
